1. Summary

LUNA is a 100% local app. Your health data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or back up to your personal iCloud / Google Drive account.

Data type Where it goes
Cycle logs, symptoms, moods, BBT 📱 Your device only (encrypted)
PIN / biometric 🔐 Device secure enclave
Notifications 📵 Scheduled locally, never to a server
Analytics, advertising, tracking 🚫 None. Zero.
Crash reports, diagnostics 🚫 None. Zero.
Device identifiers (IDFA, GAID) 🚫 Never collected

3. What We Collect

LUNA collects only the health data you enter:

  • ✅   Cycle dates, period flow, predicted dates
  • ✅   Daily symptoms, mood, energy, basal body temperature
  • ✅   Notes you write yourself
  • ✅   Notification preferences (stored locally)

We do not collect:

  • ✗ Device identifiers, IDFA, advertising IDs, GAID
  • ✗ Location data of any kind
  • ✗ Contact information, email, phone number
  • ✗ Browsing history or cross-app activity
  • ✗ Crash analytics or usage analytics transmitted externally
  • ✗ IP address (no server = no logs)
  • ✗ Any biometric data outside the device secure enclave

4. How Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device in an AES-256-GCM encrypted SQLite database (via SQLCipher), compressed with zstd. The encryption key is derived from your PIN using Argon2id (winner of the Password Hashing Competition).

// Rust core — vault.rs let key = argon2id_derive(pin, salt, memory_cost=65536, iterations=3); let db = sqlite_open_encrypted(path, key, cipher="aes-256-gcm");

Optional encrypted backup (iCloud / Google Drive) uses the same key — you must re-enter your PIN to restore. We never have access to your backup.

5. HealthKit & Health Connect

iOS — HealthKit: If you grant permission, LUNA can read/write menstrual cycle data to Apple Health. This stays on your device and is subject to Apple's HealthKit policies. Revoke any time: iOS Settings → Health → Data Access → LUNA.

Android — Health Connect: If you grant permission, LUNA can sync cycle data with Health Connect. Revoke any time: Android Settings → Health Connect → App permissions → LUNA.

Neither integration sends data to any LUNA server. There is no LUNA server.

6. Data Sharing

We never share, sell, license, or transmit your personal health data to:

  • ✗ Third-party analytics companies (Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.)
  • ✗ Advertising networks (Meta, Google Ads, etc.)
  • ✗ Social media platforms
  • ✗ Data brokers
  • ✗ Any server operated by us or anyone else
  • ✗ Law enforcement (we have no data to provide)

We cannot share what we do not have. LUNA has no database of users.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA / PIPEDA)

Since all data is local and in your control, you can exercise every privacy right directly in the app:

👁️
Access
Open the app — all data is right there
🗑️
Delete
Settings → Wipe All Data (irreversible)
📤
Export
Settings → Export Data (JSON / CSV)
🔄
Portability
Encrypted backup + CSV export
✏️
Rectification
Edit any entry directly in the app
🚫
Restriction
Disable any health integration in Settings

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, and port your data. Since all data is local, you exercise these rights directly in the app. There is no EU data protection officer to contact because no personal data is ever transmitted.

Legal basis for processing: Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — you enter data voluntarily. Special category health data: Explicit consent (Art. 9(2)(a)).

California residents: LUNA does not sell personal information. We do not "share" data for cross-context behavioral advertising. You have the right to know what personal data is collected — it is only what you type into the app, stored on your phone.

Canadian residents: LUNA collects only the minimum data necessary for the app to function. Data is stored locally and never transferred outside Canada (or anywhere). You may request access, correction, or deletion by deleting the app.

8. Children

LUNA is intended for users aged 17 and older and is rated accordingly on the App Store and Google Play. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (United States) or 16 (European Union). Since LUNA collects no data externally, no special parental consent mechanism is required.

9. Changes to This Policy

We will update this policy if LUNA adds new features that affect privacy. The "Effective date" above will change. We commit to never retroactively changing our data practices in ways that reduce your privacy.

All changes are tracked publicly in our GitHub commit history.
