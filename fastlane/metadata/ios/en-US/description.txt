LUNA is a privacy-first menstrual cycle and wellness tracker. Your health data never leaves your device.

KEY FEATURES

Cycle Tracking
Track periods, symptoms, mood, and basal body temperature. LUNA learns your unique patterns and predicts your next cycle with science-backed accuracy.

Complete Privacy
- AES-256 encryption: all data encrypted locally
- No cloud sync, no account required
- No third-party trackers or analytics
- No advertising
- Open source

4 Tracking Modes
- Regular: standard cycle tracking
- TTC (Trying to Conceive): fertile window, LH surge, BBT charts
- Pregnancy: week-by-week tracking
- Perimenopause: irregular cycle support

Health Insights
- Cycle length trends over 6+ cycles
- Symptom frequency analysis
- Mood correlation patterns
- Fertile window prediction

Optional iCloud Backup
Your data is encrypted with your personal key before syncing to iCloud. Apple cannot read it.

Accessibility
Full VoiceOver support, Dynamic Type, high-contrast mode. Available in 40+ languages.

SCIENCE-BACKED
Predictions use validated algorithms based on peer-reviewed research on menstrual cycle variability.

OPEN SOURCE
Full source code available at https://github.com/macaron-software/luna
