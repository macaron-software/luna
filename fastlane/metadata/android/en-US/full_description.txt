LUNA is a privacy-first menstrual cycle and wellness tracker. Your health data never leaves your device — encrypted with AES-256, stored locally, with no third-party access ever.

FEATURES
Period & symptom tracking with smart predictions
Basal body temperature (BBT) charting
Mood and energy tracking
4 modes: Regular, TTC (Trying to Conceive), Pregnancy, Perimenopause
Fertile window and ovulation prediction
6-cycle trend analysis and insights

PRIVACY BY DESIGN
AES-256 local encryption — your key, your data
No account required
No cloud sync (optional encrypted backup only)
No advertising, no trackers, no analytics
Open source: https://github.com/macaron-software/luna

ACCESSIBILITY
Full TalkBack support, Dynamic Text, 40+ languages

SCIENCE-BACKED
Predictions based on peer-reviewed research on cycle variability.
